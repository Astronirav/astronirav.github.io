About

I'm a Senior Research Engineer at Honda Research Institute in San Jose, CA.

My current research focus is on Spatial Perception for Embodied AI Agents, specifically focusing on Visual, natural language, auditory, and motion modalities.

I also have experience working on SLAM and Bird Eye View Perception for Autonomous Vehicles.
